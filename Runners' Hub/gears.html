GEARS

Gears are very important to runners

Proper gear, including shoes, clothing, and accessories, is crucial for distance runners. It enhances comfort, minimizes the risk of injuries, and optimizes performance. The right running shoes, weather-appropriate clothing, hydration and nutrition gear, and data monitoring tools all play key roles. Safety and recovery gear also help runners stay safe and aid in post-run recovery, ensuring consistency in training and achievement of their goals. (Click the image for more shoes' details)

Adios Pro 3 Details

The Adios Pro 3 is a long-distance running shoe with various updates and improvements from its predecessor. It offers more foam, a lower drop, and increased stack height. The shoe is slightly heavier and comes with a higher price tag.

Highlights:

Asics Metaspeed plus Detail

The Asics Metaspeed Sky Plus is a top-tier super shoe designed for fast running. It features A6ff Turbo foam for quick compression and decompression, making it a high bounce shoe that provides impact absorption. The shoe is well-suited for race days and offers enough protection for long distances. It is also suitable for workouts, particularly at marathon effort and threshold effort paces. The shoe has excellent durability, with the outsole and midsole foam holding up well after 100 miles. The upper is snug and comfortable, although it can be slightly aggressive in terms of toe curl-over.

Highlights:

  • Improved version
  • Changes in stack height and drop
  • Super bouncy but a little bit firm
